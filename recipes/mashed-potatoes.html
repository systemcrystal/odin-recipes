Mashed potatoes

Description

Mashed potato or mashed potatoes, colloquially known as mash, is a dish made by mashing boiled or steamed potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Butter
  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Boil the potatoes
  3. Add milk
  4. Add salt
