Shepard potatoes

Description

Shepherd's pie, cottage pie, or in its French version hachis Parmentier, is a savoury dish of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato and baked, formerly also called Sanders or Saunders. The meat used may be either previously cooked or freshly minced.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Beef
  • Salt
  • Butter
  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Mix together
  4. Add salt
